Chicken Soup

Recipe for Chicken Soup

Nothing screams happiness like a fresh bowl of Chicken Soup. Why go out and eat it? Lines could take hours, which means cold CHICKEN SOUP! Have it the freshest it could ever come to you...right off your stove!

This is a very easy and straight to the point recipe! Stop wasting your time, and your family's time, and get to it. Let's get cooking!

The best recipe for chicken soup is finally here.

Ingredients

  • Avocado oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Chicken
  • 2 Large Carrots
  • 2 Celery Stalks
  • Ginger
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 6 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • Salt
  • Tumeric
  • Rosemary
  • Black Pepper
  • Frozen peas
  • 1 cup pearl or Israeli couscous
  • Love

Steps

  1. Wash your dirty dishes you will use to cook
  2. Wash your dirty hands
  3. Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees
  4. Back up from the oven, it's hot
  5. Place each ingredient on your workspace
  6. Add all ingredients to a pot
  7. Heat stove to high
  8. Add MORE SEASONING
  9. Stir frequently
  10. Stir with LOVE
  11. TURN OFF STOVE IMMEDIATELY
  12. Eat the best Chicken Soup ever!!!
