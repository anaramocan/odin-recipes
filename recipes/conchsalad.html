Recipe for Conch Salad

Nothing screams happiness like a fresh bowl of Conch Salad! Why go out and eat it? Lines could take hours, which means ANGRY CONCH SALAD! Have it the freshest it could ever come to you...right off your COUNTER!

This is a very easy and straight to the point recipe! Stop wasting your time, and your family's time, and get to it. Let's get cooking!

The best recipe for conch salad is finally here.

Ingredients

  • 2 Oranges
  • Conch
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Pineapple
  • 1 Mango
  • 3 Lemons
  • 3 Limes
  • Scotch Bonnet
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Even more conch
  • Love

Steps

  1. Wash your dirty dishes you will use to cook
  2. Wash your dirty hands
  3. Place each ingredient on your workspace
  4. Chop all ingredients intensely! GET AGGRESSIVE
  5. Squeeze lemons, oranges and lime juice on each ingredient
  6. Add all ingredients to a bowl, preferably paper
  7. Add salt and pepper
  8. Stir with LOVE
  9. SHUT OUT THE WORLD
  10. Grab a Miami Vice Daquiri
  11. Eat the best Conch Salad ever!!!
