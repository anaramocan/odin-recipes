Recipe for Lasagna

Nothing screams happiness like a fresh plate of Lasagna. Why go out and eat it? Lines could take hours, which means cold LASAGNA! Have it the freshest it could ever come to you...right out of your oven!

This is a very easy and straight to the point recipe! Stop wasting your time, and your family's time, and get to it. Let's get cooking!

The best recipe for lasagna is finally here.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • More Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Even More Cheese
  • Love

Steps

  1. Wash your dirty dishes you will use to cook
  2. Wash your dirty hands
  3. Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees
  4. Back up from the oven, it's hot
  5. Place each ingredient on your workspace
  6. Add cheese cheese cheese cheese
  7. Add almost 6 feet of cheese
  8. Add Pasta
  9. Add Sauce
  10. Top with more cheese
  11. Shove it into the oven for 45 minutes
  12. Eat the best lasagna ever!!!
